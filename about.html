About

I’m Ahmed Emad Khalil — PhD researcher in quantum solid-state physics at KU Leuven/imec, working on integrated acousto-optic and piezo-opto-mechanical devices.

I design and measure electro-optic modulators, ring/racetrack resonators, and IDT-driven acoustic structures (SAW/BAW) across ferroelectric stacks on Si platforms.

You can find my work on Google Scholar and projects on GitHub.
